Introduction and overview

We do takeaway is a free and open source collection of software projects to provide a service that allows restaurants to invite customers to view their menu online, order food and then provide tools to manage the fulfillment of the order and delivery to the customer.

The original idea for this came about from Covid 19 putting pressure on restaurants to stay in business as well as wanting to compare development and use of GraphQL on a range of different back-end and front-end frameworks.

Eventually there may be a collection of blog posts and tutorials to go along with this service to help others build GraphQL applications and read about any lessons learned.
